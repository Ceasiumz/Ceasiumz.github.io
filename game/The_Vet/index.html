Game Overview

The Vet : A simulation of weighing and saving lives

The Vet is an event driven 2D RPG. You have the chance to play as Doctor Blank, a mob doctor without legit identities but do operations for the underground world.
From now on, you will face patients form every corner in this city,from
mafia members who wants to exchange kidney with his doughter, to
government agents who attempt to seek illegal organs for their superiors.
You must treat them carefully, do operations to take their organs out of their skin temporarily, or
permanently.
No matter a killer or savior, remember you are just a VET.

Gameplay Pillars

Narritive tricks & Interactive operations

diverse operation simulation

Beat the heart—or let it flatline?

For each different patient and their injuries, you may choose different situations to operate your therapy.
Open his head, Clip her chest, or Sew on its back?

  • High position-Check patient's head and face, try to find some interest informations. Or, your wanna do some *tricks* inside his brain?
  • Medium position-Check patient's neck and heart, use specialized tools to guess how blood streaming in her vein. Or, manage to *steal* the silver cross across her neck?
  • Low position-Check chest and belly, cautiously take away the origin of illness.Or, hold something *important* and refuse their retrival?

Different "Treatment" Events

To be a killer-or a savior?

The Vet follows Aya Quinn, a combat medic running contraband bio-augmentations through the quarantined vertical sprawl of Delta-12. Each choice exposes layers of corporate blackmail, rebel debts, and civilian casualties.

  • Branching dialogue that remembers every triage call.
  • Allies can abandon you if your ethics slip.
  • Flashback operations reveal why the city wants you erased.
